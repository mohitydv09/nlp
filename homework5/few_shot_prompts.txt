Question: How to tie a shoelace?
Answer: To tie your shoelaces, start by holding one lace in each hand. Cross them over each other to form an "X," then pull one lace through the bottom loop and tighten to create a basic knot. Next, make a loop (or "bunny ear") with one lace and hold it. Take the other lace, wrap it around the base of the loop, and pull it through the hole created. This forms a second loop. Pull both loops to tighten the knot securely. Adjust if necessary to ensure the bow is centered and tight enough to hold.
